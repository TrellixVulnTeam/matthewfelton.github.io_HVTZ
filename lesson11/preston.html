Preston City Covid Update

By Matthew Felton

Over the last year, everyone's lives have been forced to evolve into something very different than the typical run of a mill day to day life. With Covid we had to change and work together to protect ourselves our loved ones , to prevent them from becoming sick with this deadly virus. We now are past a full year of fighting and working to improve the living contentions back to normal like before. Vaccines are now can nowe be gotten at local offices. We currently are rquesting the elderly get their vaccines first along essential workers to help bring safety and peace of mind to those working on the front lines. We are also opening vaccines next week to manufactoring employees to help bring the economy back up to it's formal glory.
