Target Audience

The purpose of this site is to allow individuals easy access to the weather, while also giving information on events for that day locally. These events would be planned according to predicted weather with a backup plan.

Personas

  1. Jen a local mother respected mother with two children and a husband who works construction. she often worries if the weather will be safe while her husband is working. Her kids regular walk home from the bus stop and may also need protection from the elements. They don't have cable because it is is cheaper to stream Netflix.
  2. Mark stays at home and games too often, and though he has many online friends. He is looking for new activities to do outside and around town to meet new people. He wonders if the weather is better to be out and about or to look for something indoors. He is unsure where to find postings of events.

Scenarios

  1. Noelle recently started excising by walking around local parks, enlightenedday.us allows her to know if the weather will be acceptable for walking this day and if not offers other activities in the area.
  2. Saito has to get across town tomorrow for a court case, and is trying to determine how early he should leave to be early that day. enlightenedday.us provides this information.
  3. John a mild manner well rounded guy who enjoys supervising his kids soccor team. Because weather changes John needs to have a reiable way to see when it is safe to practice, and possible community team building alterative activities.
  4. Adam an enthusiast in adventure and exploring. He often hikes when the weather allows it. he often needs weather information for planning his trips for hiking. He also often enjoys meeting up and playing Dungeons and Dragons and other board games with people, and would like to have an easier way to find groups.
  5. Timmy is an average kid that no one understands, and because of this he wants to know what everyone likes doing locally so he can attempt to join in. He is unsure what to expect weather wise and wishes he could just get the anwsers in a poof.
